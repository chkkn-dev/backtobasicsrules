Server Rules

Back to Basics Roleplay

Welcome to Back to Basics Roleplay! Please read and adhere to the following rules to ensure a fun and fair experience for everyone.

General Rules

1. Be respectful to all players and staff.
2. No cheating, exploiting, or using hacks.
3. No spamming or advertising in chat.
4. No inappropriate or offensive language or behavior.
5. Follow all roleplay guidelines and respect the roleplay environment.

Roleplay Rules

1. Always stay in character during roleplay scenarios.
2. No random killing (RDM) or random arresting (RDA).
3. No metagaming or using out-of-character information in roleplay.
4. Respect other players' roleplay and do not disrupt ongoing scenarios.
5. Use common sense and play fair.

Building Rules

1. No building in public areas or obstructing pathways.
2. Do not abuse props or create prop spam.
3. Follow server guidelines for base building and structure.
4. Do not exploit building mechanics to gain an unfair advantage.
5. Keep builds realistic and within the roleplay theme.
